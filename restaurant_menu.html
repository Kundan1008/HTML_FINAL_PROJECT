Bhaji Plate

Bhaji, also known as pakoras, are crispy deep-fried dollops made from spicy chickpea batter. They are typically served with spicy condiments called chutney. Varieties include onion bhaji (thinly sliced onions added to the chickpea batter) and bhajis made from cauliflower, potato, and paneer. Price: 30 rs

Samosa Plate

Samosas are a popular Indian street food. They consist of a small amount of spicy potato or meat filling wrapped in dough and deep-fried until crispy and flaky. Price: 60 rs

Vada Pav Plate

Vada pav is essentially a spicier vegetarian version of sliders. It features deep-fried fritters or flattened patties of potato sandwiched between two halves of a dinner roll (pav). Condiments like onions, green chilies, garlic chutney, and mint-cilantro chutney are often added. Price: 10 rs
